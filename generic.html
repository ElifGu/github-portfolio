About me

As a tech enthusiast making a transition into the field of data, I bring comprehensive management experience. My particular passion lies in storytelling to present complex scenarios in an engaging manner for all stakeholders, contributing to data-driven decision-making.

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Elif, a Berlin native with a unique background in Sinology, Chinese Language, and Art History. With over a decade of experience in adult education and management, I've refined my competences in various international settings, notably while serving as the Erasmus Project Coordinator and Vice Director of the International Office at Anadolu University in Turkey.

My journey led me to a recent data analyst bootcamp, where I rapidly gained skills in Python and libraries (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, SQLAlchemy, Seaborn, Plotly etc.), SQL (MySql, PostgreSQL), and data visualization tools (Tableau). My strong customer service background, coupled with my newly acquired data analytics skills and leadership experience, positions me as an ideal candidate to bridge the gap between management and IT.

Eager to delve into the world of data, I look forward to contributing my analytical skills and passion for learning to a dynamic team. Join me on this exciting new chapter!
